Keyboard Controls
“Esc” – Closes the running application.
“X” – Enables the wireframe. 
“C” – Disables the wireframe. 
“1-4” – Used to pick one of the four cameras.
“W” – Moves the Plane forward.
“S” – Moves the Plane backwards. 
“A” – Moves the Plane left. 
“D” – Moves the Plane right. 
“R” – Moves the Plane upwards. 
“F” – Moves the Plane downwards. 
“Q” – Rotates the Plane left. 
“E” – Rotates the Plane right.
